Who am I and what do I do?

My name is Lars Leenders.
I'm a Dutch 21 years old and I'm currently studying Creative Media and Game Technologies at Rotterdam University of Applied Sciences.
I love being outdoors, gaming and classic nerdy stuff such as TTRPG's and LARP.

Education so far

  • Christelijk Lyceum Delft Molenhuispad, Gymnasium-Technasium plusklas
  • Sint Stanislas College Pijnacker, HAVO (NG+NT profiel)
  • The Hague University of Applied Sciences, Mechatronics (2 years)
  • Rotterdam University of Applied Sciences, Creative Media and Game Technologies (current)

Work

When I turned 13 my father told me it was time for me to get a job. Part of my upbringing or so he said.
At the time I obviously wasn't happy with the prospect of having my free time stripped away from me.
Now however I can see how much this has brought me.
The range of jobs I've had over the years meant dealing with different kinds of people and different kinds of responsibilities.
It has also taught me good insight in problem solving, customer/guest satisfaction, some basic electrical- and mechanical-engineering, Teambuilding and lastly endurance.

  1. Casa Del Sol, Tanthof --- Dishwasher and (unofficial) souschef
  2. Golfbaan Delfland, Delft --- Drivingrange Employee - Drivingrange Manager
  3. Dok/OPEN Bibliotheek, Delft --- Kitchen staff (basically dishwashing again)
  4. Phimex Warehousing B.V., Rotterdam --- Warehouse and logistics Overseer and Employee
  5. Stadsherberg De Mol, Delft --- Workfloor Manager or as we call it:''Waardgezel''
